## 🌟 Overview

**EL MESÍAS** is a premium Next.js web application built purely as a fan tribute to the greatest footballer of all time. It steps away from standard encyclopedia formats, offering instead a deeply immersive, cinematic, and interactive journey through Messi's life and career.

The project leverages a robust local JSON database to dynamically render everything from his childhood roots in Rosario, to his unparalleled 91-goal year, to his crowning glory in Qatar.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Cinematic Parallax Engine:** Deeply immersive scrolling experiences using `framer-motion` to create layered, hardware-accelerated parallax effects across hero images and scatter galleries.
- **Albiceleste Aesthetic:** A custom design system utilizing Argentina Sky Blue (`#75AADB`) and Sun of May Gold (`#F6B40E`), layered over a sleek `slate-900` dark mode canvas.
- **Glassmorphism UI:** Heavy use of modern web design trends, including frosted glass cards (`backdrop-blur`), subtle glow effects, and precision hover micro-interactions.
- **Comprehensive Database:** 100% data-driven pages powered by an extensive suite of local JSON files covering:
  - Career Statistics & Records
  - Goal Archives & Matches
  - Trophy Cabinets (Ballon d'Ors, World Cup, Champions League)
  - Personal Biography, Family Tree, and FIFA Profiles
- **Responsive Architecture:** Fully optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop viewing without compromising on animation fluidity or layout integrity.

## 🗺️ Page Structure

- **`/` (Home):** The cinematic entry point featuring the World Cup triumph, iconic celebration showcases, and top-level career statistics.
- **`/about` (Biography):** The Albiceleste Biographical Nexus. Details his heritage, physicality, EA FC ratings, family tree, and global sponsorships.
- **`/matches` & `/goals`:** Deep dives into historical data, cataloging his greatest performances and net-busting moments.
- **`/awards` / `/trophies`:** The ultimate trophy cabinet visualizing a career of absolute dominance.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Framework:** [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router)
- **Styling:** [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- **Animations:** [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/)
- **Icons:** [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)
- **Deployment:** Vercel (Recommended)
